About Us

ScanText PDF is a smart mobile application that converts scanned images into clean, readable PDF files.

  • 📷 Scan documents using camera
  • 🧠 Extract text using OCR (Apple Vision AI)
  • 📄 Convert extracted text into formatted PDF
  • 💾 Save and share instantly

Privacy Policy

ScanText PDF does not store your scanned data on external servers. All processing is done locally on your device for maximum privacy and security.
