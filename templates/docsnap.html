Privacy Policy

DocSnap is designed with privacy in mind. PDF processing happens locally where possible, and only the extracted text needed for summarisation is sent to OpenAI using the API key provided by the user.

📁 Local First

Your PDF files are selected from your device and are not stored permanently by DocSnap.

🔑 Your API Key

Your OpenAI API key is used only to generate summaries and can be stored locally on your device.

🚫 No Tracking

DocSnap does not sell your data or track your personal documents.
